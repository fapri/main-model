Web Application for FAPRI-MU - Food and Agricultural Policy Research Institute Marketing Strategies

This is a web application for concise display of our current marketing strategies.
The banner at the top displays each of our strategies, an aggrate of the results, and a dictionary of terms we have used.
The "base model" is our most basic model in which other strategies are built upon. The "multi-year" model incorporates multi-year sales into our base model.

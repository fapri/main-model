Version Descriptions

Price Objective

  • Our base model
  • 10% PO sales

Price Objective V2

  • 20% PO sales at 90th percentile

Price Objective V3

  • July 20th liquidation date (June 20th for corn)
  • Each postharvest PO sale is at 20% of whatever is left on Sept 1st

Price Objective V4

  • 10% PO sales
  • July 20th liquidation date (June 20th for corn)

Price Objective V5

  • 20% PO sales at 90th percentile
  • July 20th liquidation date (June 20th for corn)

Price Objective V6

  • Functions off of base model
  • 50% of crop must be sold on or before first day of post-harvest
  • 10% PO sales

Price Objective March

  • The first 30% of pre-harvest crop is sold on the December futures. The remaining 20% of crop is sold on the March futures
  • July 20th liquidation date (June 20th for corn)

Price Objective March Baselines

  • All sales are triggered from the March Baselines
  • Basis is adjusted in March and August
  • July 20th liquidation date (June 20th for corn)
